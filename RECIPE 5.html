COCONUT TARO DELIGHT

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 medium taro
  • 20g desicated coconut
  • 1 tbs of coconut milk
  • pinch of salt

METHOD

  1. Peel taro,wash and put it on fire
  2. Mash it when cooked
  3. Add desicated coconut and mix
  4. Add coconut milk and pinch of salt
  5. Mix well and pipe it into desire shapes
  6. bake and serve
