CORNISH PASTIES

INGREDIENTS

  • 100g of pastry flour
  • 50g of fat
  • 1 small carrot
  • 1 small onion
  • 50g of meat

METHOD

  1. Wash vegetable and cut into cubes
  2. cook the meat
  3. Add meat and vegetable in a pan and prepare filling
  4. Sift flour in a mixing bowl
  5. Add fat to flour and rub in with your finger tips
  6. Add 2 to 3 spoons of water to the mixture
  7. Mix it, roll and cut
  8. Add filling and mould
