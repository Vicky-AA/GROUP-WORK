SHORTBREAD BISCUITS

INGREDIENTS

  • 150g flour
  • 100g fat
  • pinch of salt
  • 50g sugar

METTHOD

  1. Measure 150g of flour into a mixing bowl
  2. Add fat to the flour
  3. Rub in with your finger tips
  4. Add sugar and salt to the mixture
  5. Add 2-3 spoons of water to it
  6. Dust the board with flour
  7. Roll and cut into shapes
