JOLLOF

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g of meat
  • 1 medium onion
  • 50g of tomatoes puree
  • 100ml of oil
  • 1 cup of rice

Method

  1. Pour oil in a pan
  2. Cut onions and add to oil
  3. Put it on fire ,add meat and tomatoes to it
  4. Add salt and let it simmer for 30 minutes
  5. Wash rice and add to stew
  6. Add 1 cup of water
  7. Stir and leave it on fire for 20 minutes
  8. serve when cooked
